Design Principles : Lesson 02

White Space and Clean Design


I feel like the first site I think of when I think of white space is Apple. They do such a good job of having the perfect balance of a clean white space while still using it to accentuate their products perfectly.

Visual Hierarchy

As soon as you open the Conde Nast site, you immediately see graphics that really highlight what the company loves and promotes. They do an amazing job of really showcasing that right off the bat and having a very strong visual site.

Rule of Thirds


Rolling Stone is a great example of the rule of thirds. They have immediately featured 3 different graphics for different stories of different topics and also have at least three different categories of stories and links to different topics that showcase the rule fo thirds perfectly.
